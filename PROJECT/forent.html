TIC TAC TOE

RULES:

Tic-tac-toe is a game for two players. They take turns putting 'X' or 'O' in a 3x3 grid. The goal is to get three in a row. The game ends when someone wins or the grid is full.

SNAKE

RULES:

Control growing snake on a grid, eat for points, avoid collisions. Increasing speed adds challenge. Restart after a game over. Classic appeal in simplicity and variations.

FIND ME

RULES:

In the memory card game, players flip pairs to find matches, earning extra turns. Variations include adjusting difficulty and custom rules; the winner has the most pairs.

TYPING MASTER

RULES:

Never look at the keyboard, even for symbols and case changes. Focus on accuracy to build confidence and efficiency. Use index keys (F and J) and practice consistently
